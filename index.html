1.

LANDING PAGE QUALITY

Without landing page quality, nothing else works. Landing page quality is like an on/off switch, you either have it or you don't. If you don't, you'll invariably have very low quality scores (1/10). With such QS, trafic in almost inexistent and costs are too high for anything to work. Landing page quality affects both search quality scores and display network quality scores.
